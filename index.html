At a Glance

Bitcoin vs Monero

Two paths from the same origin — transparency vs privacy.

Bitcoin (BTC)

Transparent public ledger — every transaction visible
Pseudonymous addresses easily linked by chain analysis
Embraced by TradFi, ETFs, and institutional custody
Fixed supply of 21M with diminishing block rewards
Privacy requires third-party tools (CoinJoin, mixers)
vs

Monero (XMR)

Private by default — senders, receivers, and amounts hidden
Ring signatures, stealth addresses, and RingCT built-in
Resists surveillance — IRS $625K bounty still unclaimed
Tail emission ensures perpetual miner incentive
Fungible: every coin is equal, none can be blacklisted
