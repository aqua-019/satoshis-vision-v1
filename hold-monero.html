🛡️ Why Monero?

Monero (XMR) is the only major cryptocurrency with privacy by default. Ring signatures, stealth addresses, and RingCT ensure your transactions are truly private.

  • Untraceable transactions
  • Unlinkable addresses
  • Fungible currency
  • ASIC-resistant mining

📖 Best Practices

When acquiring Monero, follow these guidelines to maximize your privacy:

  • Use a fresh receiving address for each swap
  • Wait for full confirmations before considering funds safe
  • Consider using Tor or VPN for additional privacy
  • Store XMR in your own wallet (Feather, CLI, or Cake)
  • Never reuse addresses or share view keys unnecessarily
