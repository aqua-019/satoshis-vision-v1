Storage best practices
Self-custody principles
Write down your seed phrase
25 words. Never digital. Never in a screenshot. Store on paper or metal in 2+ locations. This IS your wallet.
Use your own node
Remote nodes work but leak your IP. Running your own node is the privacy gold standard. ~180GB disk space.
Privacy is default
No mixing. No extra steps. Every Monero transaction uses ring signatures, stealth addresses, and RingCT automatically.
Hardware wallets
Ledger and Trezor both support XMR. Use Feather Wallet or Official GUI as the interface. Keys never leave the device.
